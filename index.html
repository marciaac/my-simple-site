A little bit about me.

I am a seasoned graphic designer, with experience in
print design, production, illustration and digital projects.
As a self-motivated, strategic thinker who thrives both independently and in a team setting, I have a proven track record for translating complex ideas into compelling design solutions on time and within budget. I recently expanded my skills into web design, and am excited to be armed with this new knowledge and ready to put my new skills to work.

Whether it's creating a high quality piece while maintaining corporate design standards, or allowing my creative juices
to flow freely, the end result will be an eye-catching design
that communicates clearly and effectively. Design specialties include publications, brochures, eblasts, invitations, direct mail, digital graphics, and websites. My textile collection and other licensed designs can be found at Swizzlestix or at the social media links below.
